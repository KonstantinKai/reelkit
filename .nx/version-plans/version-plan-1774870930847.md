Signal-driven rendering — controls, navigation, and wave loader update independently without full component re-renders. Wave loader for image and video loading states, backed by content preloader to skip cached media. Nested slider forwards loading callbacks to inner slides and clears loader on video→image navigation. Video frame captured before transition for seamless poster display. onReady/onWaiting added to SlideRenderProps and NestedSlideRenderProps for custom slide loading control. BREAKING: SoundState removed — import SoundProvider and useSoundState from @reelkit/react instead.
